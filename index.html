Road to WUCC

Anchor was established in 2016 with the goal of competing at the highest level of mixed gender club ultimate. With a 10th place finish at the Canadian Ultimate Club Championships (CUCC) 2016 and a 1st place finish in 2017, we continue to rise and take our goal to new heights. We are currently training to compete at the World Ultimate Club Championships (WUCC) 2018 in Cincinnati, Ohio.
